The website to support the Runoff for Russia (RFR v1.0) dataset. Here we present a collection of interactive plots of projected changes of runoff and its characteristics for 425 river basins across Russia.

Development of the presented web-service was funded by the Russian Foundation for Basic Research (RFBR) according to the research project No. 19-35-60005.
